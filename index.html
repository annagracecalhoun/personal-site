Anna Grace Calhoun


I was born in Boston, MA (go Redsocks!!) where I lived for 6 years before moving to Charlottesville, VA. I'm the oldest of 4 siblings and have the best dog ever, Louie. I enjoy spending time outside, doing things like hiking or skiing. Some of my favorite experiences have been travelling to new places and learning about different cultures, which I feel very grateful to have been able to do.

As a 2nd year at the University of Virginia, I'm studying computer science, public policy, and economics. I'm interested in data science and how combining technological tools and economic frameworks in innovative ways can improve the outcomes of public policy. Outside of school, I'm involved in Forge where I helped teach a data science course and now co-direct the Impact program, which organizes students to do data science, user experience, and marketing project for nonprofits. I'm also on the Student Council Legislative Affairs committee, where I work on increasing civic engagement and lobbying on behalf of the student body.
